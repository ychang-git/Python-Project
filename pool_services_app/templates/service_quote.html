Services

Summer (start of season)

  • winter cover removed and stored
  • all equipment will be assembled and operational
  • entire pool will be brushed, vacuumed and the chemistry balanced
  • equipment will be inspected
  • any issues will be reported to the homeowner
    • (excessive chemicals will be charged accordingly)

    Weekly Cleaning and Maintenance services

    * on a day of your preference

  • Water will be balanced
  • pool vacuumed, skimmed, brushed, and saftey cover closed(if applicable)
  • circulation equipment serviced, filter area/mechanical pit will be maintained
  • weekly service includes all basic chemicals for balancing water.
  • consistent access to the pool and equipment is required

    • Winter (end of season)

  • pool will be lowered
  • water will be super chlorinated
  • all circulation piping will be blown out and plugged
  • equipment will be dis-assembled and stored
  • heater will be winterized and treated to limit rodent nesting
  • winter cover will be installed(if applicable)
    • equipment will be inspected and any issues will be reported to the homeowner
